What We Do

We offer one-on-one English conversation classes for advanced English speakers.

Real life situations rarely offer long pauses for us to think and gather our thoughts. As an advanced English speaker, it’s imperative that you challenge yourself to be your best so that you can hold a conversation and speak English fluently in real time. You need to have advanced listening skills and the confidence to respond immediately. To help you achieve this, we meet with you online for 55 minutes. During your class we share a dialogue. We can discuss your goals, family or life in general. We can focus on an area you want to improve in or whatever you feel comfortable talking about. Whatever your topic preference, this experience gives you the opportunity to speak English fluently with a native English speaker while we guide the conversation when necessary to help you stay on track to attaining English fluency.

We also take notes of the errors you make when speaking during the meeting. Then we send them to you afterwards with corrections so that you can review your opportunities for improvement and learn from your mistakes.
